Distinguished Professors

Professor Najeeb Jung is the former Lt. Governor of Delhi, who was the Vice-Chancellor of Jamia Millia Islamia University. Sh. Jung has served in the Asian Development Bank and has been a key driver of energy research and transformation in Asia.Apart from his work in energy, he is a noted translator and poet, his latest work being a masterful translation of Mirza Ghalib. The SGT fraternity will benefit from his guidance in setting up an interdisciplinary research program on Asia Studies.
Prof. Najeeb JungFormer Lieutenant Governor
Mr. Indu Bhushan, a former Indian bureaucrat and economist, served as the first CEO of Ayushman Bharat, revolutionizing healthcare across India. With degrees from top institutions, including Johns Hopkins University, he held key positions at the World Bank and the Asian Development Bank. Mr. Bhushan emphasized the need for more hospital beds in rural areas while managing costs and advocated for digital reforms. We're proud to have such an accomplished individual as the vice chairperson of ASIA at SGT University.
Sh. Indu BhushanVice Chairman, ASIA
Sandhya is an Independent Director, mentor, and start-up/scale-up ecosystem partner. As a senior global executive in financial services, she excels in strategic business model innovation, digital transformation, and social impact. She serves as an Independent Director on multiple boards, including Electronica Finance Limited and TTK Prestige. She also serves on the board of IIIT Delhi, TiE Bengaluru, and Social Venture Partners Bengaluru. Her diverse experience contributes unique perspectives to foster growth and change. SGT is proud to have her on board.
Ms. Sandhya VasudevanMember of the board, ASIA
Professor Dinesh Singh is an Indian professor of mathematics, having served for over 4 decades as an academic in institutions in India and abroad. A Padma-Shri awardee, he has often been felicitated for his work in his field. He received the Career Award in Mathematics of the University Grants Commission and the AMU Prize of the Indian Mathematical Society. Professor Singh has always been known to foster research and innovation wherever he has gone. SGT is proud to have him as a mentor and wisdom.
Prof. Dinesh SinghDistinguished Professor
Mr. Rajat M. Nag is an internationally renowned development professional specializing in emerging markets, development issues, strategic visioning, and regional cooperation. He has held several high-ranking positions at the Asian Development Bank (ADB), including the role of Managing Director-General. Mr. Nag's educational background includes degrees from IIT and the London School of Economics. His expertise has solidified his reputation as a leading figure in international development. We're proud to have him as part of our team at SGTU.
Sh. Rajat M. NagMember of the board, ASIA
